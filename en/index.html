Our Vision And Mission

Vertex is the extreme point where two lines intersect.

This is only Vertex's meaning in mathematics; in anatomy it is the highest part of the body and mind, in astronomy the zenith of the universe, in space geometry the most fundamental building block, in network theory the central node where all key connections merge, in optics the absolute focus where light intersects the lens.

We took our name from the collective meaning formed by all these meanings and more; as humans, we will show the highest level of our potential and limits, and reach the peak together!

In line with this vision, we are happy to launch Vertex Events; our mission is to unite people in line with this vision, to grow in the technology sector and to develop the sector together.
